Our vision

We believe the intention of trading mustn't be exclusively to make a profit. As traders, we have the power to invest in companies that have better solutions for our planet. Veggie-stock's mission is to make that more accessible to the average investor.
For every £100 you invest through our app, we plant a tree. By doing that alongside using renewable energy to power our business, we ensure we have a negative carbon footprint.
